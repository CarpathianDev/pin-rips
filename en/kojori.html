Kojori-Korogli Castle trip

In our opinion presented destination has power to engage every curious nature lover. Kojori/Korogli is one of best hike options in close vicinity to Tbilisi. When you spend exciting moments in hustle bustle banks of Tbilisi’s Mtkvari river, Kojori castle detour will bring you chill.

While on the trip, you will get a chance to explore unexplored rocky fortress. Kojori has its fame of historical place well attached to the Georgia’s past days. Historians assume that this spot had a status of royal court summer palace.

Be first before archaeologists (castle was not yet scientifically evaluated) and sink inside extremely picturesque landscape seen from royal ruins. From here, especially in autumn, overwhelming views will allow you as observer to see vast, areas of SE part of Sakartvelo/Georgia. Craggy hill tapped with crown created by partially preserved medieval castle walls, stands behind small village famous from its forests and fresh air.
