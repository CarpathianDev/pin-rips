PiN Trips

Trip in Sakartvelo (Georgia)- 14 days

Day 1

Fly to Sakartvelo=airport in Kutaisi, hostel in Kutaisi, Breakfast -Gelati and Bagrati churches, Prometheus Cave, Stay in Kutaisi;

Day 2

Breakfast in Kutaisi – Gori (Stalin Museum and Gori Castle) – Uplistsikhe cave town – hostel in Tbilisi;

Day 3

Breakfast in Tbilisi- Walk in Old Tbilisi streets - Narikala fortress – Dry bridge antique market- Hostel in Tbilisi;

Day 4

Breakfast in Tbilisi- Ethnographic Museum and picnic in the museum garden – Georgian National Museum - Evening visit in Sulphur Baths – Night stay in Tbilisi;

Day 5

Breakfast in Tbilisi – Mtskheta=Old Capitan of Kartli, Jvari Monastery, Svetitskhoveli, Armaztsikhe- Kazbeg/Stepantsminda, visit to Holy Trinity Church - Night stay near Stepantsminda, in mountain village Phelshe;

Day 6

Breakfast in Phelshe, Truso gorge, stop in Jvari viewpoint – Ananuri fortress – Kakheti – degustation of a local wine – place of Love Sighnaghi – Hostel in Sighnaghi;

Day 7

Breakfast in Sighnaghi – Telavi – Tsinandali Palace – Gombori pass – stopping at Gombori pass for views and tea – Tetritskaro – overnight in Tetritskaro;

Day 8

Breakfast in Tetritskaro – Archaeological site Samshvilde – Tsalka lake and Dashbashi canyons, picnic near the canyons- come back to Tetritskaro;

Day 9

Breakfast in Tetritskaro – Walk in Bolnisi village with German houses and church with oldest Georgian inscriptions – Dmanisi, place of first hominids – Back to hostel in Tetritskaro;

Day 10

Breakfast in Tetritskaro – Day in a village, how people are living, their everyday life. Opportunity to bake bread, make cheese and other Georgian food, having dinner with local family. Back to Tetritskaro;

Day 11

Breakfast in Tetritskaro – Back to Tbilisi, from where is a train to Batumi (6 hours) – check in at the hostel - walking in the city centre;

Day 12

Breakfast in Batumi – botanical garden – Gonio, Gonio fortress – overnight in Gonio;

Day 13

Breakfast in Gonio beach – mountainous Adjara - walking in the villages – overnight in Khulo village;

Day 14

Breakfast in khulo – Mtirala national park – Petra fortress – overnight in Kutaisi;

Day 15

Fly from Kutaisi;

Trip dates: September 2020. It is also possible individual appointment.
